# Sales and Profit Dashboard (Power BI)

## Project Overview

This project focuses on analyzing sales and profit performance using an interactive Power BI dashboard. The aim is to understand how revenue and profit vary across regions, product categories, and customer segments, and to identify key business trends.

The dataset was cleaned and transformed before building the dashboard to ensure consistency and accuracy in analysis.

---

## Dashboard Structure

### 1. Sales Overview

This page provides a high-level view of revenue performance.

It includes:

* Total Revenue indicator
* Sales trend over time
* Top 5 products based on revenue
* Revenue distribution by region
* Filters for year, region, and segment

---

### 2. Profit Analysis

This page focuses on profitability insights.

It includes:

* Total Profit indicator
* Average Profit Margin
* Profit by category
* Sales vs Profit comparison across regions
* Profit contribution by customer segment

---

## Tools and Technologies

* Power BI for dashboard creation
* Basic data cleaning and transformation
* Data visualization techniques for business insights

---

## Key Insights

* The West region generates the highest revenue and profit
* Technology category contributes the most to overall profit
* Furniture category shows relatively low profitability
* Consumer segment accounts for the largest share of profit contribution
